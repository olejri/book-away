# VoiceDraft

## Speak your idea. Send it anywhere.

VoiceDraft lets you record your voice, transcribe it, and send a draft as an email to any address — whether that's a project board, a colleague, or any email-based workflow.

### Features
- 🎙️ Voice recording & transcription
- 📧 Send drafts to any email address
- 🏷️ Custom labels & members
- 🌙 Dark mode, mobile-friendly UI

### Getting Started
1. Sign in with Discord or Slack
2. Add one or more destination email addresses in **Settings**
3. Go to **Dashboard**, record your voice, review the draft, and send
